How does it work?

When you join Vote Swap, you'll be matched with a friend in your Facebook network. Friends in swing states that want to vote Stein or Johnson will swap votes with friends in safe states that want to vote Clinton. This lets third parties get votes without giving away the presidency to Trump.

Is this legal?

Yes! In Porter v Bowen (2007), vote swapping websites were ruled legal, at least in the 9th circuit.

On the merits, we hold that [California] violated Appellants’ First Amendment rights. The websites’ vote-swapping mechanisms as well as the communication and vote swaps they enabled were constitutionally protected.

For more background, please read "The Ninth Circuit ruled that vote-swapping is legal. Let’s use it to stop Trump" by Scott Aaronson, or electionlawblog.org's coverage of the ruling.
